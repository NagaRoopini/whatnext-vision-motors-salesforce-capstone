# WhatNext Vision Motors: Shaping the Future of Mobility with Innovation and Excellence

## 📌 Project Overview
This Salesforce Capstone Project focuses on digital transformation of WhatNext Vision Motors by
automating sales, customer management, and service operations using Salesforce CRM.

## 🏢 Business Problem
The company faced challenges in:
- Managing customer data manually
- Tracking vehicle sales and service requests
- Generating reports for management decisions

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Automate customer and vehicle data management
- Improve sales pipeline visibility
- Enhance customer service experience
- Enable data-driven decisions using reports and dashboards

## ⚙️ Salesforce Configuration
- Custom Objects: Vehicle, Dealer, Service Request
- Standard Objects: Account, Contact, Opportunity
- Automation: Flows, Validation Rules
- Security: Profiles, Roles, Permission Sets
- Reports & Dashboards

## 🧪 Use Case Flow
1. Customer registers interest in vehicle
2. Sales team creates opportunity
3. Service requests tracked post-sale
4. Management views performance dashboards
